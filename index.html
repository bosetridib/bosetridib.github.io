Abstract

I am a Doctoral Student in Economics at the University of Helsinkia. I was a Subject Matter Expert I graduated from Banaras Hindu University with an MA in Economics (specialization: Econometrics).

My research area is Time-Series Econometrics, and currently, I am researching on Macroeconometrics of Climate Change. I have completed several online courses in R, time-series econometrics, and data-science concepts. I have developed independent projects in volatility and covid-19 analysis on GitHub.

I am proficient in R and Python and intend to use data-science tools in my research. In the future, I see myself pursuing a research career in macroeconomic and financial modeling with such methods.
